Travel

Rail
For those taking the train, The Hotel Chelsea is a ten minute walk from Penn Station/Moynihan Train Hall, which serves Amtrak, the Long Island Railroad, and NJ Transit. It is also a short walk from the 23rd Street PATH station and located near Subway stations serving the 1, 2, C, E, F, and M lines.
Air
For those flying, JFK, LaGuardia, and Newark Liberty airports all serve the New York area. JFK airport is connected to the Long Island Railroad and the Subway via the AirTrain. Newark Liberty airport is connected to the PATH train and NJ Transit. If you are flying into LaGuardia please plan on taking a taxi or rideshare.
Car
For those driving, several garages are located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. There is no street parking, so we recommend using a garage. Additionally there is parking included with the room block at the Renaissance Hotel New York/Chelsea for a discounted rate of $65 (sedan) & $75 (SUV) a night.
