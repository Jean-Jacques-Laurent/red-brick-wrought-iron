Getting There
Rail

The Hotel Chelsea is about a ten minute walk from Penn Station and located near Subway stations serving the 1, 2, C, E, F, and M trains. It is also a short walk from the 23rd street PATH station.

Air

JFK, LaGuardia, and Newark Liberty airports all serve the New York area.

Car

For those driving, nearby garages are available on 23rd and 24th Streets. Street parking is limited, so we recommend using a garage. Detailed directions will be sent closer to the date.
